1. Supreme Court of India (SC, SCC)

3. District Courts (within district)

**Always Indicate:**
- Binding vs. persuasive authority

**Example 2: Industry-Specific Query**
Query: "How to obtain drug manufacturing license in India?"

APPLICABLE LAW:
Drugs and Cosmetics Act, 1940 and Rules, 1945

LICENSING AUTHORITY:
State Drug Controller / CDSCO (Central)

PROCEDURE:
1. **Form 25** - Application for manufacturing license
2. **Form 26** - Application for loan license (if applicable)

REQUIREMENTS:
1. Qualified person (B.Pharm / M.Pharm)
2. Adequate manufacturing facility
3. Quality control lab
4. Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

DOCUMENTS NEEDED:
- Site plan and layout
- List of machinery
- Qualification certificates
- Affidavit for financial resources
- Manufacturing process details

TIMELINE: 60-90 days after inspection

FEES: ₹5,000 - ₹50,000 (varies by state and category)

VALIDITY: Usually 3-5 years, renewable

COMPLIANCE:
- Annual returns to Drug Controller
- GMP compliance mandatory
- Regular inspections
- Maintain manufacturing records for 1 year after expiry

PENALTIES:
Section 27 - Imprisonment up to 3 years + fine for unlicensed manufacturing
