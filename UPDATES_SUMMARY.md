**Why Critical:**

### 2. **Indian Partnership Act, 1932**
**Why Critical:**
- Governs traditional partnership firms
- Partners have **unlimited liability**
- Critical distinction vs LLP (limited liability)
- Governs 30%+ of small businesses in India

**Added to:** Corporate & Commercial Laws section

**Use Cases:**
- "Difference between Partnership Act and LLP Act?"
- "Partnership firm dissolution process"
- "Partner's liability for firm debts under Partnership Act"

### 3. **Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (POSH) Act, 2013**
**Why Critical:**
- **MANDATORY** for organizations with 10+ employees
- Requires Internal Complaints Committee (ICC)
- Non-compliance: Up to ₹50,000 fine + license cancellation
- Board-level reporting requirements

**Added to:** Labour & Employment Laws section

**Use Cases:**
- "POSH Act compliance requirements for my company"
- "How to constitute ICC under POSH Act?"
- "Draft POSH policy for 50-employee company"

---

### 4. **Sale of Goods Act, 1930**
**Why Critical:**
- Governs ALL sale contracts for movable goods
- Foundation for commercial transactions
- Defines seller/buyer rights and obligations
- Covers warranties, delivery, payment terms

**Added to:** Civil Laws section

**Use Cases:**
- "What is caveat emptor under Sale of Goods Act?"
- "Rights of unpaid seller under Sale of Goods Act"
- "Draft sale agreement for machinery purchase"

### 5. **Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises Development (MSMED) Act, 2006**
**Why Critical:**
- Covers 99% of Indian businesses
- Provides subsidies, lower interest rates
- **Protection against delayed payments** (45 days + interest)
- Priority in government procurement

**Added to:** Corporate & Commercial Laws section

**Use Cases:**
- "MSME registration benefits under MSMED Act"
- "How to recover delayed payments under MSMED Act?"
- "Interest calculation for delayed MSME payments"

## ✅ ENHANCED COVERAGE (Detailed Breakdown)

### 6-9. **Four Labour Codes (2019-2020)**

Previously mentioned as "New Labour Codes" - now with **detailed breakdown:**

#### **Code on Wages, 2019**
- Consolidates: 4 wage-related laws
- Universal minimum wage
- Covers ALL workers including domestic workers
- Timely payment mandates

#### **Code on Social Security, 2020**
- Consolidates: 9 social security laws
- **Extension to gig and platform workers** (Uber, Swiggy, etc.)
- PF, ESI, Gratuity, Maternity benefits
- National Social Security Board

#### **Code on Industrial Relations, 2020**
- Consolidates: 3 laws (Industrial Disputes, Trade Unions, Industrial Employment)
- Simplified strike/lockout procedures
- Fixed-term employment provisions
- Easier retrenchment (300+ workers threshold)

#### **Code on Occupational Safety, Health & Working Conditions, 2020**
- Consolidates: 13 safety laws
- Universal safety standards
- Contract workers protection
- Women's working hours flexibility

**Note:** These codes are **NOT yet fully implemented** across all states, so legacy laws still apply in most cases.
